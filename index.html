The focus app built for students

Fócas helps secondary school students voluntarily limit distracting apps during study and school hours — building focus as a habit, not enforcing it as a rule.

🛡️
Student-led You choose what to block and when. No one controls your phone but you.
👨‍👩‍👧
Family support Share your progress with a parent or guardian who can cheer you on.
🏆
Build streaks Earn badges, track your focus score, and hit your weekly goals.
🔒
Privacy first All data stays on your device. We never inspect your phone or collect personal data.
